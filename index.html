1. Information We Do Not Collect

Simple Times Table does not collect any personal data. This includes:

  • Personal information
  • Name, email, or contact information
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Audio recordings

The app runs entirely offline. No data is stored or transmitted.

2. How the App Works

Simple Times Table helps young learners practice multiplication using audio-based learning. The app includes:

  • Sequential times table playback
  • Random shuffle mode
  • Q&A mode with pauses for answers
  • Adjustable speech speed and volume

All features operate fully on the device with no external communication.

3. Children’s Privacy

This app is designed with children in mind and complies with COPPA principles:

  • No data collection from children
  • No account creation or login required
  • No ads or external links
  • No tracking technologies

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external SDKs. No information is shared with any external party.
